# Bay Area Contractor Outreach — Launch Seed Supply

> Goal: line up a credible roster of real Bay Area contractors who consent to a
> founding-member profile on RenovateConnect, so the app looks alive on day one.
> This is the "single-player supply magnet" from
> `MARKET_ENTRY_AND_PROFITABILITY_PLAN.md` §Phase 0 made concrete.
>
> **Cold-start reality:** a marketplace with zero supply converts no one. Seed
> supply is a *launch dependency*, not a post-launch task.

_Last updated: 2026-06-04_

---

## 1. Who to target (and who NOT to)

**Best early "yes" candidates — prioritize these:**
- **Independent design-build remodelers** (1–15 employees). Hungry for qualified
  leads, own their brand, decision-maker answers the phone.
- **Kitchen/bath specialists** — high-ticket, photogenic portfolios that make the
  app's gallery look great.
- **Solo licensed contractors & quality handymen** — fastest yes, happy for any
  free exposure, fill out the category breadth.

**Deprioritize for launch:**
- National lead-gen-saturated giants (they're jaded by Angi/Thumbtack and slow).
- Firms with no web/photo presence (nothing to seed a profile with).
- Anyone we can't license real photos/reviews from (consent matters — see §5).

**Selection bar:** active CA CSLB license, real project photos available, ≥4★
on at least one platform (Yelp/Houzz/Google), serves a Bay Area metro we're
launching in.

---

## 2. The offer (why they say yes)

A **free Founding Contractor** listing — frame it as scarcity + status:

- ✅ **Free verified profile** with top search placement at launch (no lead fees,
  ever — that's our anti-Thumbtack differentiator).
- ✅ **"Verified Pro" badge** + a spot in the launch "Verified Pros" carousel.
- ✅ **We do the setup for them** — import their photos and reviews, write the
  profile. Zero effort on their end.
- ✅ **Free AI estimate tool** their own customers can use.
- ✅ **First N founders locked in** (e.g. "first 20 contractors in the East Bay").

Ask in return: permission to list them, a few portfolio photos, and (ideally) a
testimonial or willingness to take the first few leads.

## 4. Outreach playbook

**Channel priority:** (1) warm intro if any, (2) Instagram DM for IG-active
remodelers, (3) personalized email, (4) phone call to the office. Solo
operators: text/call. Aim 30–50 contacts to land 10–15 founders.

### Email template
> **Subject: Free founding spot for [Company] on a new Bay Area renovation app**
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I came across [Company]'s work on [Houzz/Yelp/IG] — the [kitchen/bath/project]
> photos are exactly the quality we want to feature.
>
> We're launching **RenovateConnect**, a Bay Area app that connects homeowners
> with vetted contractors. We're hand-picking a small group of **founding
> contractors** to feature for free at launch — top placement, a Verified Pro
> badge, and **no lead fees**. We'll set up your whole profile for you from your
> existing photos and reviews; it takes you zero time.
>
> Could I get your OK to feature [Company], and a few project photos to include?
> Happy to send a mockup of how your profile would look first.
>
> [Your name] · RenovateConnect

### Instagram DM (shorter)
> Hi! Love [Company]'s remodels 🔨 We're launching a Bay Area renovation app and
> hand-picking a few founding contractors to feature free at launch — top spot,
> verified badge, no lead fees, we build the profile for you. Mind if we feature
> you? Can send a mockup first.

### Phone script (opener)
> "Hi, is this [Name]? I run RenovateConnect, a new Bay Area renovation app. We're
> featuring a handful of top contractors for free at launch — no cost, no lead
> fees. I'd love your permission to feature [Company]; we handle all the setup.
> Can I email you a mockup of your profile?"

### Objection handling
- *"Is this another Thumbtack?"* → "Opposite — we never charge per lead. Founding
  listing is free, top placement, you only ever pay a small % if you close a job
  through the app."
- *"I'm too busy / not taking work."* → "No setup effort on you — we build it.
  And you control your availability in the app; turn off leads anytime."
- *"What does it cost later?"* → Be honest: free founding listing now; future
  revenue is the 8% deposit commission on jobs booked through the app.

## 5. Consent & compliance (do this right)

- **Get explicit permission** before any business appears in the app — that's the
  whole point of this outreach. A short written OK (email reply "yes, go ahead")
  is enough to start; keep a record.
- **Don't scrape and repost** Yelp/Houzz reviews or photos without rights — ask
  the contractor to supply their own photos, or get written permission to use
  specific ones. Reviews should be re-earned in-app, not copied.
- **Use real, accurate info** — verify CSLB license # and that it's active; don't
  imply endorsement they didn't give.
- **Offer easy opt-out** — "tell us anytime and we'll remove your profile." Builds
  trust and avoids complaints.
